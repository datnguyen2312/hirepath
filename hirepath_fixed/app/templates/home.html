WHY IT WORKS

A focused workflow students can actually act on

HirePath is not a job marketplace. It is a preparation tool that helps students understand how their resume lines up with one target role, what to improve first, and how to get interview-ready.

  • Starts with visible resume evidence from the student
  • Benchmarks against real role requirements and readiness signals
  • Turns analysis into concrete resume and interview actions
  • Keeps the scope focused on preparation, not hiring automation
RESPONSIBLE AI

Guidance only

HirePath supports preparation and self-improvement. It does not make hiring decisions.

Explainable output

Role-fit insights are based on visible resume evidence and target-role skills.

Skill-focused

Feedback emphasizes skills, evidence, and readiness rather than shortcuts or appearances.

Action-oriented

Each step leads to a clear improvement action the student can take next.

FOUR STUDENT-CRITICAL QUESTIONS

What HirePath helps answer in one flow

Am I qualified? Role-fit analysis shows where you already have evidence and where the gaps remain.
What should I fix first? Resume feedback prioritizes the language, evidence, and bullets to improve next.
How do I get interview-ready? Practice one mock interview response and get clear coaching on how to improve.
What should I do next? Receive a simple action plan with resume, skills, and interview priorities.
